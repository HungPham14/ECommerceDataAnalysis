SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

Definition: the process of analyzing digital text to determine if the emotional tone of the message is positive, negative, or neutral. 
Today, companies have large volumes of text data like emails, customer support chat transcripts, social media comments, and reviews.

How-to-do: Download free dataset to train predictive model, using mathematics algorithms such as XGBoost, Logistic Regression, etc

Brainstorming: Since we cannot analyze tone of audio which could make out of many different emotion such as sadness, happiness, satisfying, etc. 
We have to use a dictionary of words to predict the classification of the sentences (positive, negative, neutral).
